About

Hi! I'm Mattia Rizzo

I am a software developer with a passion for making (and playing) videogames.

Based in Modena, Italy, I graduated in IT Engineering in 2016 and I worked as a Software Engineer and Mobile Developer for three years.

In November 2019 I enrolled in a First Level Master Degree in Game Development at the University of Verona to pursue my dream of becoming a Game Developer.

What I love of making games is the opportunity to unleash creativity while having technical challenges at the same time.

I have worked on numerous projects, mainly using the Unity game engine, where I have been able to tackle various aspects of game development.

The one I am most familiar with is the network synchronisation and the multiplayer related features, but I also find it interesting to manage the user interface.
